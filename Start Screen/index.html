Trench Run

It is a period of civil war. Rebel spaceships, striking from their base on the moon Yavin 5, have started their first battle against the evil Galactic Empire. The plan is to destroy the Empire’s ultimate weapon, the DEATH STAR, an armored space station with enough power to destroy an entire planet. During the battle, the legendary star pilot NAMEHERE has been chosen to attempt a dangerous trench run that will lead to the only vulnerablility of the death star and restore freedom to the galaxy….
